Graduation 2023 - GOOD LUCK!!!

The wonderful May weekend is slowly coming to an end... For most of us, it's a very relaxing three days, but for fresh graduates, it's a stressful time...
Dear Seniors!!!
We hope you have rested and have a supply of energy for the next few days! You have an important exam ahead of you, but you are definitely ready and we suggest you prepare a few things now, so as not to be unnecessarily stressed tomorrow morning! First of all, set your alarm clocks and be at school with an adequate supply - 30 minutes. before the exam is really a very good time!!! You don't come to the designated rooms at the last minute - remember that after drawing a place and leaving your belongings (then there is time to check if you have everything :), you can still leave the room / hall.

Read more

  • stock of black pens,
  • ID card (also great for cutting the sheet :),

Constitution Day May 3

This year marks the 232nd anniversary of the adoption of the Constitution of May 3 - the first written constitution in Europe and the second in the world (after the US Constitution). The holiday was established in 1919 and then again in 1990.

Read more
The Constitution Day on May 3 commemorates the creation of the first Polish law that regulated the system of the Republic of Poland. It was the second such document in Europe and the third in the world. The act changed the state system into a hereditary monarchy and led to the political equalization of townspeople and nobility. It also introduced the protection of the state over peasants, which was supposed to mitigate abuses in the collection of serfdom. Under the Constitution, power was divided into legislative, executive and judiciary.
