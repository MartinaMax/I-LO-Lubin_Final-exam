Doors Open in Copernicus!!

Already on May 26, we invite our younger friends and colleagues to close meetings with Copernicus :)

The Open Doors start at 13.00 and, as usual, Copernicus in a nutshell is waiting for you :) Eighth graders will have completed their tests, so there's no better way to start the weekend! Team Copernicus at Predator Games 2023!

On March 31 - April 27, 2023, the representation of our school took part in the National Predator Games 2023 Competition. The competition was held online and consisted of 4 games: League of Legends, Rocket League, Chess and Fortnite.

Read more

The representatives of our school in individual games were : Wojciech Bąk 2A, Kacper Mikita 2B, Paweł Kanigowski 2B, Szymon Majerczak 3A, Filip Kozyra 3A, Michał Grygielski 3B (League of Legends), Przemysław Mazurowski 3A, Michał Wojtanowski 3A, Rafał Raś 3A, Hubert Żelewski 3B (Rocket League), Bartholomew Rog 1E, Kacper Werner 4A (Chess), Jakub Ochocki 1B, Bartosz Drążek 2B, Konrad Kozicki 2B, Igor Jakubowski 2C (Fortnite), The greatest success is Bartek Róg , who took 15th place in chess out of 1024 starters!!! Congratulations to Bartek and the other students and we wish you success in the next year.
