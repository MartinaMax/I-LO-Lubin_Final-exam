About I LO

In the 2023 "PERSPEKTYWY" ranking our school placed:

12th in Lower Silesia and 163rd in Poland in the overall ranking.

10th in Lower Silesia and 152nd in Poland in Matura ranking.

Our school promotes both the attainment of knowledge, as well as the development of talent. We continuously strive to cultivate a spirit of acceptance and amity that helps our students thrive as individuals.

Our promise

We make sure that our school serves as an environment that allows for personal growth. As our student you will have many opportunities to:

Develop your talents

Whether it's art, music, sport, or anything in-between, we hold many activities and workshops that will allow for you to grow your abilities. We also hold competitions and public events for when you're ready to showcase your talent to a larger audience.

Learn something new

We always strive to offer an expansive curriculum in all subjects, so that even the most ambitious of students can expand their knowledge. For those reaching above the standards, we also offer extracurricular activities that can help you prepare for your chosen college degree.

Be a Part of something great

We pride ourselves on our school being a diverse community brought together to form fond memories. Together, we work to organize events, workshops, and trips to create a one of a kind experience.
